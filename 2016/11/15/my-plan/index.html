My plan

2016/11/15

I disagree with President-elect Trump on many matters of style and substance, but I agree with him that America needs to tackle a number of long-standing problems. In the spirit of constructive criticism, I offer a simple plan that I think will restore balance to our economic system while spurring growth and creating jobs.

It’s based on a simple principle: Everybody pays. No person who lives in this country and no business that operates here should avoid paying federal income tax. Everyone needs a stake in the game.

My plan is this. Establish a minimum level of federal income tax that every individual or joint filer must pay. Establish a minimum level of federal income tax that every business operating in the country must pay.

I suggest a penny, or really 1% of gross income – not adjusted gross income where deductions and credits can muddy the waters. Once everyone has ponied-up, we can talk about what kinds of tax incentives we need to spur job creation and retention, and how we grow the economy and address the challenges of climate change. But, let’s start simple: Everybody pays.

Patriots can surely pony-up a penny.
