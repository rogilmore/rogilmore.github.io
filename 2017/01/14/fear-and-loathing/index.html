fear and loathing

2017/01/14

fear and loathing
mouthparts foaming
eyeballs rolling
twitter trolling

heaven, hell
who can tell
prideful swell
what’s that smell

red or blue
false or true
in a stew
what to do

forward back
email hack
ceiling crack
fiction fact

smaller words
most absurd
loins to gird
anti-nerd

seek the sun
american
race not won
work ain’t done
